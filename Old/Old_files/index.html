About

Hey there!, I am a First year Master's Candidate at the University of Michigan School of Information specializing in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). I am currently seeking UX/Product Design internship for Summer 2017.

I have constantly been challenging myself to expand my knowledge in HCI at the grad school by participating in various events and doing eccentric projects ranging from web design to tangible and physical computing designs. For me, Design is more about people and their emotions rather than the product itself.

I had an epiphany while working for Ernst and Young for almost two years. I realized that people across the organization had different needs for the same platform. This realization made me wonder how designing a user-centered product can profoundly impact the productivity and efficiency of any organization.
