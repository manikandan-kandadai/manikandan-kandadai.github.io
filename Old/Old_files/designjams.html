About

Hi, I am a First year Master's Candidate at the University of Michigan School of Information specializing in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). Being an Engineer turned into a Designer, I have always been fascinated about the confluence of Design and Technology. Particularly, I am interested in critically reflecting on design decisions to analyze the impact these decisions are making in the lives of Users. Having said that, these analysis have helped me in shaping my critical thinking skills to look for alternative approaches to improve the User Experience and Interaction.


To further develop my Critical Thinking and Making skills I will be taking the "Integrated Product Development" course this Winter which is jointly organized by UofM Ross School of Business, UofM School of Engineering, UofM Stamps School of Architecture, UofM School of Information and UofM Tauber Institute of Global Operations.
