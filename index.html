Monster Together

Tangible Interaction, Fluid Interfaces

Entending physical gameplay to digital world by tracking physical tokens on a interactive touch table. This allows us to share attributes of the physical tokens to the digital space, thereby enhancing the experience.

A study is being planned with children of ages 6-8 years.

Digital Kalimba

Tangible Interaction, Acoustic tracking

An intervention at creating a musical instrument that works using a smartphone’s IMU (Gyroscope + Accelerometer) sensors and microphone to digitally modulate the music.

The experience of creating digital music is enhanced by the affordance provided by the physical instrument module.

Watch Demo

VR Ninjak

Mixed reality UX, Body tracking

An exploration into creating interventions for natural user experience in Virtual Reality gameplay. For the prototype, body tracking using Kinect was used to detect interactions.

Google Cardboard SDK with Microsoft Kinect and Node.js made the magic happen.
