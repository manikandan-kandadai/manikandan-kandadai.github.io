About

Hellooo, I am a First year Master's Candidate at the University of Michigan School of Information specializing in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). I am actively seeking internship for Summer 2017.

Being an Engineer turned into a Designer, I have always been fascinated about the confluence of Design and Technology. Particularly, I am interested in designing digital and physical interfaces for users to have a seamless and intuitive experience. Having said that, I constantly look for alternative approaches thereby enhancing the User Experience and Interactions by creating unique experiences for the users.
