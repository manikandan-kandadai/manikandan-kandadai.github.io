Overview




About the Problem:

This Design Problem deals with creating a solution that will help women in India connect with their friends and family when in need and also be more socially responsible by being aware of their rights.

Target Users

Women living in India (20-35 years)


More about the Problem:

The safety of women is a global concern that technology alone cannot solve. However, a growing concern is that most of the women at large are unaware about the Laws and Legislations that are available to protect them, particularly in developing nations where there is a burst of population and technology. This makes most of the women unaware about their social responsibilities and do not excersise their right to freedom of speech and expression.
