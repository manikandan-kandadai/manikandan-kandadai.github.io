    About Me

    Hi, I am Mani, a Master's student at the University of Michigan, studying Human-Computer Interaction. I am currently looking for full-time opportunities beginning June 2018 or later.

    I like to experiment a lot in life and take risks. With a Bachelors in Electronics Engineering, I branched out to be a cybersecurity consultant with Ernst & Young, thereby learning about security and ethical hacking. Two years back, I decided to move on with life and pursue another passion of mine - Design. In the past year, I have been exploring design as a service, product, and as an experiment. I have worked on social computing applications, tangible interfaces, Virtual Reality, mobile and web application designs. Currently, I am working as a research assistant at the usable privacy group at UM where I am investigating privacy that is usable for people by designing better interaction modalities for products and services.

    I enjoy everything Sci-Fi (novels, movies and TV Series [read ‘Rick and Morty’]). Fan of DC Comics (Batman FTW!). Always wanted to learn an instrument, started with a Ukelele recently.

    If you are a recruiter and is looking for someone who can quickly learn and adapt while walking on thin ice, I am your guy. My ability to take risks have always placed me in uncomfortable situations and at foreign places. But these experiences have made me more flexible and knowledgeable across a variety of domains.

    Lets talk more about how my experiences can assist you with your requirements. Shoot me an email! Thanks for stopping by, have a great day!
