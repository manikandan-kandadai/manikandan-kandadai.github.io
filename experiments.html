EXPERIMENTS

An exploration in to Physical, Virtual and Mixed spaces

Tangible Interaction 

Blending the physical and digital spaces (kinect+unity+creative coding)

An attempt at bringing digital gameplay to tangible touch surfaces which can detect unique tokens and can manipulate them in the gameplay. An extended study is planned by experimenting with kids and young adults.

Watch Demo

Kalimba

A musical instrument for your smartphone? (woodworks + Programming)

An intervention at creating a musical instrument that works using a smartphone’s IMU sensors and microphone to digitally modulate the music using the smartphone’s accelerometer and gyroscopes.

Watch Demo

VR Ninja

Natural Interaction for VR games (Kinect + Node.js)

Can VR experience be enhanced by integrating natural interactions with VR object? This was a prototype demonstration to understand the effectiveness of such an interaction. Google Cardboard SDK with Kinect and Node.js made the magic happen.
